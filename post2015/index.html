ABOUT Kosovo's POST2015 Debate


Kosovo was privileged to be selected to contribute to the POST2015 debate. The Innovations Lab Kosovo led a series of consultations with young people, through street action, online polling, SMS and university lectures asking three main questions:

What is your biggest dream in the next 10 years?
What is wrong with the world today that you most want to put right?
What are the 3 things that you would most want to change?

The data are presented as a slopegraph that shows the connections between youth and issues they have raised. The three main questions are highlighted at the top of the page and each one is visualized with a different graph.

Each graph is divided into two columns: showing the municipalities of Kosovo on the left--along with the corresponding number of youth respondents categorized by gender, age cohort--and the issues they have raised on the right. The relative thickness of the lines between the two columns represents the number of respondents identifying a given issue.
