ABOUT Kosovo's POST2015 Debate


Kosovo, as one of the privileged few countries was selected to contribute to the POST2015 consultations led by the United Nations Kosovo Team. The Innovations Lab held a series of consultations with young people, primarily through street action, online , SMS and university lectures asking three main questions:

What is your biggest dream in the next 10 years?
What is wrong with the world today that you most want to put right?
What are the 3 things that you would most want to change?

The data are presented as a slopegraph that shows the connections between youth and issues they have raised. The three main questions are highlighted at the top of the page and each one is visualized with a different graph.

Each graph is divided into two columns: showing the municipalities of Kosovo on the left, with the corresponding number of youth respondents categorized by gender with age groups, and the issues they have raised on the right. The thickness of the lines between the two columns represent the number of young people who have raised issues to be most important to them.
